Megen Nelson

Writer and Digital Media Generalist

Bio

Megen holds a B.A. in English Literature from the University of Central Florida and has done work in technical writing and editing with NASA, instructional design with 21CLS, and creative and freelance writing and design.

Megen is currently pursuing a graduate degree in Digital Media, with a focus on interactive education.

When Megen's not working, she can be found reading, playing video games, watching movies, looking at pictures of cute animals, hanging out with family and friends, or working on one of her many creative side projects.

Writing

Megen has been a professional technical writer since 2013. Most of her work has gone into online web courses for universities, or engineering documentation for NASA and other aeronautical programs as a contractor.

She helped write documentation about NASA's ground systems, such as the Space Launch System (SLS), and the Underway Recovery Test 7 (URT-7) for the Orion capsule. She has also assisted in working launch documentation for missions such as Crew Demo-1 and Crew Demo-2 for SpaceX, as well as the launch of the Perseverance rover to Mars.

Most of that work is confidential and not available to the public, but she is happy to answer questions and provide references as needed. Below you will find a sample of work that is available to the public.

Instructional Design

During a college Experiential Learning program placement in 2013, Megen began her role as a Course Developer at 21st Century Learning Solutions. She deeply enjoyed creating online education. She has experience analyzing courses for higher education institutions that must align strictly to accreditation guidelines, while also casually designing education for self-guided and group-led learning.
